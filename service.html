OUR SERVICES

PRIMARY CARE

Whole person care that fits your life.

Whether you're feeling under the weather or just want routine visits — it's an ongoing relationship built on trust, access, and understanding. We care for adolescents through older adults, offering comprehensive services that include annual physicals, chronic disease management (like diabetes, hypertension, and high cholesterol), medication management, and more.

We believe great care takes time. That’s why your appointments are never rushed, and you’ll always have the space to ask questions and share concerns — whether you're dealing with short-term symptoms or long-term care.

WOUND CARE MANAGEMENT

Specialized healing, brought to your doorstep.

Healing complex wounds requires more than basic wound dressing — it demands knowledge, precision, and consistency. At Healthy Highways, we provide advanced wound care services for patients recovering from surgery, living with diabetic ulcers, pressure injuries, or venous insufficiency wounds.

We come to you — offering high-quality care in the comfort of your home. Our approach combines evidence-based techniques with individualized treatment plans to promote healing, prevent infection, and reduce complications.

CARE COORDINATION

Keeping Your Health on Track — Without the Stress.

Managing your health can feel overwhelming — especially if you see multiple specialists, take different medications, or have chronic conditions. That’s where we step in.

Our Care Coordination service ensures all your healthcare providers are on the same page. We help you:

  • ✅ Understand your treatment plans
  • ✅ Keep track of medications and refills
  • ✅ Coordinate lab work, imaging, and specialist referrals
  • ✅ Follow up on results and next steps
  • ✅ Assist with transportation arrangements when needed

SAME DAY TELE-HEALTH VISIT

Quick, compassionate care when you need it most.

Getting sick can be stressful — finding care shouldn't be. At Healthy Highways, we offer same-day sick visits to help you get the answers and relief you need, fast. Whether you’re dealing with a sore throat, cold, flu symptoms, stomach issues, a minor infection, or just not feeling like yourself, we make it easy to connect.

You can choose a virtual visit or, when appropriate, an in-person evaluation. We’ll assess your symptoms, offer treatment options, send prescriptions to your pharmacy, and provide clear guidance so you know exactly what to do next. No waiting days for an appointment. No urgent care shuffle. Just timely, thoughtful care that works with your life.
